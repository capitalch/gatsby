Philips BT-50A/00 2.1 Wireless Bluetooth Speaker (Green/Yellow)

Hear It Loud and Better

The Philips BT-50A/00 2.1 Wireless Bluetooth Speaker (Green/Yellow) are ideal to playback high volume and distortion-free music with its salient Anti-Clipping functionality. You can enjoy a high grade acoustic quality with crisp, clear sound for even the loudest of your tracks. Whatever be the remaining battery status in your BT-50A/00 2.1 Wireless Bluetooth Speaker, it accepts input signals between 300 mV-1000 mV to give you audio without any intrusions or scattering. Your musical highs remain within the proximity of the Wireless Bluetooth Speaker's amplifier-range to effectively avoid post-clipping interference with sound letting you listen distortion-free loudness.

No More Messy Cables

Make simple Bluetooth connections with your smartphone, tab, MP3, PC and other audio systems without any fuss of tangles and cords. Ideal to play your music when on the fly, the BT-50A/00 2.1 Wireless Bluetooth Speaker is light weight and compact for enhanced ease of carriage. With its built-in rechargeable battery, the Wireless Bluetooth Speaker provides you with substantial periods of active run.

Arresting Colours

Available in vivid black, white/grey, green/yellow and red/grey colour versions, the BT-50A/00 2.1 Wireless Bluetooth Speaker complement your style. The modish look and the cylinder-like shape fascinates you and makes the Wireless Bluetooth Speaker your perfect music partner.

Wireless Music Streaming

Anti-Clipping Function

Anti-Clipping lets you play music louder and keeps the quality high, even when the battery is low. It accepts a range of input signals from 300 mV to 1000 mV and keeps your speakers safe from damage by distortion.
